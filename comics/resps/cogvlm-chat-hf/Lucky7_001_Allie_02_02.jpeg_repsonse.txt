In this vintage comic strip, a young boy named Allie is excitedly chasing after two girls, but is stopped by his father who playfully teases him. The father warns Allie that if he continues to chase girls, his dog will bite him. Allie, eager to please his father, agrees to stop chasing girls. However, in a twist of humor, the father reveals that he was just teasing and that there was no real threat from the girls.
