In this vibrant comic strip, a lion magician named Leo, wearing a red and white striped suit and a tall red hat, is performing a magic trick involving a saw. He promises to cut a cat in half but then reveals a cat inside a green box. As he prepares to cut the box, a white cat appears, and the audience, including a pig and a dog, watches with anticipation. The audience's reactions range from excitement to surprise. The comic is filled with playful interactions and colorful illustrations, capturing the essence of a live magic show.
