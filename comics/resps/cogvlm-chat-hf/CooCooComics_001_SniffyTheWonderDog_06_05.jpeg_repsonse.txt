In this comic strip, a man is excitedly discovering a box in his attic, thinking it might be a treasure. As he opens it, a dog jumps out, causing him to trip over a trunk. The man is surprised and asks the dog what's inside the box. The dog, seemingly mischievous, hints that there's something valuable inside, like silverware. The man, intrigued, decides to check the contents of the box, but before he can, the dog playfully runs away, leaving the man in a state of anticipation.
